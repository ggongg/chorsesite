Our Mission

Meet C-Horse, prorgamming language for children. C-Horse is easy to use and and learn. It is a great way to introduce children to programming.

Our Features

Currently our programming language has the following features. If you have requests for other features or general feedback, please feel free to reach out to us by filling out the form at the bottom of the page.

  • Simple Math & Operations

    Ability to use C-horse to calculate simple math equations. Ability to calculate additional operations.

  • Making Decisions and Repeating Actions.

    Making Decisions and Repeating Actions based on the given conditions provided by the user.

  • Remembering & Recalling Things

    Ability to remember and recall things. (variables)

  • Repeating Actions and Remembering Recipes

    Repeating Actions and Remmebering Recipes. Follow Recipes.
